In my mechatronics class, a robot competition was held where students had to build robots that collected more cubes than their opponent within 60 seconds. Using a blend of programming, circuitry, and CAD, the robot that my team built features sensors to detect the borders of the playing field to prevent it from falling off of the field and arms that gather the cubes.
